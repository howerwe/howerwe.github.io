Our work on objects
Pinned Objects

Pinned Objects compose of insect specimens affixed by pin to trays, each accompanied by one or more paper labels containing collection metadata.

Labels cannot be removed without risk to fragile specimens, and overlap with insect bodies and manual handling is slow, limited to ~2 trays per person per day.

We use the “ALICE” multi-camera oblique imaging system plus OCR to capture specimen & label images simultaneously—no label removal required.

For this process we believe that it will take about a full year to digitise all objects with it costing about $14,000 AUD.
